# AI Lab Revenues: OpenAI + Anthropic + xAI

## Summary
We predict combined annualized revenue for OpenAI, Anthropic, and xAI will reach **$60B** (10th: $35B, 90th: $90B) by December 31, 2026. This prediction uses a three-model approach: (A) company projections weighted 45%, (B) hyper-growth startup reference class weighted 35%, and (C) a slowdown/ceiling scenario weighted 20%. The combined revenue grew 4.7x YoY from Dec 2024 ($6.6B) to Dec 2025 ($30.8B), but we expect growth to decelerate as companies face the historical challenge of sustaining 2-3x growth at scale—no company has ever grown from ~$10B to $100B in 3 years.

### Reference Class: Hyper-Growth Startups
Historical precedent suggests extreme growth rates are hard to sustain:
- Fastest historical examples (Tesla, Meta): 7 years to go $10B → $100B
- Typical hyper-growth: 1.5-2x annual, not 2-3x sustained
- AI may be exceptional, but skepticism warranted at scale

## Model Framework

### Model A: Company Projections (45% weight)
Based on stated targets and growth multipliers:
- OpenAI: $20B × 2.3x = $46B
- Anthropic: $26B (stated target)
- xAI: $0.8B × 3x = $2.4B
- **Total: $74B**

### Model B: Reference Class (35% weight)
Assumes growth decelerates to typical hyper-growth (1.7x):
- OpenAI: $20B × 1.7x = $34B
- Anthropic: $10B × 1.7x = $17B
- xAI: $0.8B × 2x = $1.6B
- **Total: $52B**

### Model C: Slowdown/Ceiling (20% weight)
Assumes model improvements slow, reducing demand growth (1.3x):
- OpenAI: $20B × 1.3x = $26B
- Anthropic: $10B × 1.3x = $13B
- xAI: $0.8B × 1.5x = $1.2B
- **Total: $40B**

### Weighted Calculation
0.45 × $74B + 0.35 × $52B + 0.20 × $40B = **$59B ≈ $60B**

## Adjustment Factors

### Upward pressures
- Unprecedented enterprise AI adoption
- Strong product-market fit (ChatGPT, Claude)
- Competition driving aggressive growth targets
- API revenue growing alongside consumer products

### Downward pressures
- Historical precedent: no company has achieved this growth trajectory
- Model improvements may plateau, reducing demand
- Economic conditions / enterprise budget constraints
- Increasing competition from open-source and smaller players
- xAI uncertainty due to X merger (no longer reporting separately)

## Final Prediction
**Central estimate:** $60B
**10th percentile:** $35B
**90th percentile:** $90B

## Methodology Notes
- Three-model approach with explicit weighting
- Model A (45%): Company projections—optimistic but companies have track record
- Model B (35%): Reference class—typical hyper-growth deceleration
- Model C (20%): Ceiling scenario—accounts for model improvement slowdown risk
- Bounds widened for out-of-model factors (xAI uncertainty, macro conditions)
- Data sources: Epoch AI Companies database, survey baseline estimates
